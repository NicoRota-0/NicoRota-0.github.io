About Me

Hello! I'm currently working as a research scientist, I am specialised in the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) within the field of biomolecular engineering. My work is fundamentally committed to enhancing the design and synthesis of biomolecules, with a particular focus on proteins and RNA. My principal responsibilities and achievements include the design and implementation of LLMs. I manage projects aimed at the development and optimization of LLMs for producing biomolecules that possess specific functional properties. This process requires a seamless integration of bioinformatics, computational biology, and machine learning to devise models that can predict and construct innovative structures of proteins and RNA.
